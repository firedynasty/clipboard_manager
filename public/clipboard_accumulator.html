How it works Click Append Clipboard to grab your clipboard. Content with --- separators auto-splits into individual snippets. Use checkboxes to select snippets, then Copy Selected for just those, or Copy All for everything. Each snippet has its own Copy and Delete buttons. Cmd+2 → type a to send collected clipboard here automatically.
